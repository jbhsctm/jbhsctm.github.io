Our Story

About Us

Joel Barlow High School's CTM (Connecting Through Music) is a student-run ensemble at Joel Barlow High School. We perform at resident homes and related venues, sharing our love and joy of music.

🎵 All Instruments Welcome
🎓 Student-Run
🏠 Community Focused
